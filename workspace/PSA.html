Humans and animals around the world suffer due to oil spillage and chemical pollution, causing many diseases such as Cancer, AIDS, and stomach flu. One attempt to solve this problem was the Waste Shark, which can clean up trash on the ocean's surface. However, the Waste Shark cannot clean up chemicals or clean below the level of the ocean. We plan to create a drone capable of cleaning both trash and chemicals from underwater and the water's surface Our drone will have to be able to resist dangers such as water pressure and the ocean's chemical composition. We plan to deploy our drone at Dockweiler Beach due to the nearby Hyperion treatment plant, and the fact that Dockweiler Beach has been shut down before due to trash pollution. Any chemicals detected will also be reported to the LA County Environmental Collection Center.
